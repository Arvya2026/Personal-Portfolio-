Enclaraa — Agentic Legal AI, Built by VenRAAG

Enclaraa is the flagship Legal AI platform I founded and architected under VenRAAG. It brings Agentic Retrieval-Augmented Generation into the day-to-day workflow of law firms, in-house legal teams and compliance functions — through a layered architecture: secure on-prem document ingestion, hybrid vector & keyword retrieval, domain-tuned language models and evaluator-optimiser agents that verify their own output before it ever reaches the user.

Traditional RAG systems hallucinate on dense legal text because they treat every document the same. Enclaraa is built differently: retrieval and generation are fine-tuned on legal-specific corpora, every response carries citation-level traceability, and agentic verification runs over the top. The result — every answer, clause or draft ships with provenance the legal team can trust and defend in front of partners, regulators or a court.

Arvya Tech: AI Invoice Extraction & Customised AI

At Arvya Tech, I lead the deployment of AI invoice extraction and custom LLM applications for enterprise clients. The invoice engine combines large language models, computer vision and bespoke parsers to read multi-format invoices, purchase orders and receipts — extracting line-item detail without manual templates and flowing straight into the client's ERP or accounts-payable pipeline.

The outcome: finance teams stop re-keying data, exception queues shrink, and compliance inherits a clean, auditable trail of every document processed. More broadly, Arvya Tech partners with enterprises to find where custom AI actually moves a KPI — then ships production-grade systems on AWS and Azure with full CI/CD, observability and on-call readiness.

Recognition

Team Enclaraa were crowned winners of the global LegalTechTalk Hackathon — judged by QuantumBlack (AI by McKinsey) and Cleary Gottlieb — after shipping a real-time AI contract assistant under 48-hour pressure and outperforming teams from leading institutions worldwide. That victory became the launchpad for Enclaraa's commercial release and its place among the Top 50 global legal-tech startups shaping the next wave of AI transformation.

Alongside the hackathon win, VenRAAG was awarded the Generator Bursary by Santander Universities and the University of Greenwich, and selected for the Mountside Ventures Investment Readiness Programme (Cohort 7) — independent validation of the research-led approach behind Enclaraa.
