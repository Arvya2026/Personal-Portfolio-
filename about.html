Founder · CEO · AI Architect

Building Agentic AI for the legal industry — from London & Vijayawada

I help law firms and in-house legal teams reclaim the hours they lose to drafting, research and document review — without ever sending their data outside their own walls. As Founder & CEO of VenRAAG, I lead the team behind Enclaraa: a purpose-built Legal AI platform with live visual citations, batch document analysis and on-prem deployment for full data sovereignty.

My conviction is simple — in regulated work, AI has to be a force multiplier, not a black box. Alongside VenRAAG, I serve as Director of Arvya Tech (Vijayawada), where we ship enterprise-grade LLM applications, Gen AI systems and cloud-native automation for organisations that need to move past pilots and into production.
