Founder · CEO · AI Architect

Building Agentic AI for the legal industry — from London & Vijayawada

As CEO & Founder of VenRAAG, I lead our mission to solve the legal industry's productivity crisis by eliminating repetitive, manual work through purpose-built AI. Our product, Enclaraa, helps legal teams operate faster with greater consistency and accuracy — reducing time wasted on drafting, research and document handling while keeping data fully under the client's control.

At VenRAAG we're committed to a smarter, more efficient legal future where technology is a force multiplier, not a replacement. Alongside VenRAAG, I serve as Director of Arvya Tech (Vijayawada), engineering enterprise-grade AI systems and applied Gen AI for organisations that need to automate workflows, unlock insights and accelerate decision-making.
